SpellVPN User Guide Privacy Policy
Before contacting support:

SpellVPN requires a compatible user-provided configuration. Pro is paid and ad-free; Ultra is free with ads and optional App Store subscriptions.

Common Questions

Why does connection fail?

Check that your imported configuration is valid, that the server is reachable, and that iOS VPN permission was approved.

Which SpellVPN edition has ads or subscriptions?

SpellVPN Pro is a paid download with no ads or in-app purchases. SpellVPN Ultra is free, can show Yandex/AdMob ads, and offers optional auto-renewing subscriptions to remove ads and unlock Ultra features.

Can SpellVPN provide a server or subscription?

No. SpellVPN is a client. It does not sell, host, or recommend VPN servers.

Where are my configs stored?

Configs and logs are stored on your device unless you export, share, or back them up through iCloud Drive.
