ArtCut User Guide Privacy Policy

Common Questions

Where are projects stored?

Drafts and generated media caches are stored locally on your device unless you export or share them.

Which ArtCut edition has ads?

ArtCut Free can show Yandex/AdMob ads around editing, feed, app-open, and export workflows after ATT status is resolved. ArtCut Paid is a paid download with no ads and does not initialize advertising SDKs.

Does ArtCut currently use subscriptions?

The current paid/free project setup is ad-supported free plus paid ad-free. If a later build enables membership or premium template subscriptions, purchase terms and privacy disclosures should be updated before release.

Why does automatic captioning require permission?

Automatic captions use Apple's speech recognition framework when you start caption generation.

Why did export take a long time?

Reverse clips, overlays, transitions, mosaics, speed changes, and high-resolution media can require additional on-device rendering time.

How do I clear storage?

Use the app's cache cleanup tools to remove generated renders and temporary files.
