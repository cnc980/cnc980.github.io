Apple Ledger Privacy Policy

Common Questions

Where is my finance data stored?

Finance data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, it syncs through your private iCloud account.

Does Apple Ledger upload data to a developer server?

No. The current version has no developer-operated finance-data server and no third-party analytics SDK.

How do I protect the app?

Enable the privacy lock in settings. Authentication is handled by Apple system services such as Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode.

How can I move data?

Use JSON backup and restore for full app data, or CSV import and export for transaction workflows.
