SpellVPN Privacy Policy

A paid VPN client for advanced users who import their own sing-box configuration, subscription, proxy link, QR code, or file.

SpellVPN Support

Data We Collect

The current version of SpellVPN does not collect personal data, does not include advertising SDKs, does not include third-party analytics SDKs, does not create user accounts, and does not track users across apps or websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

SpellVPN stores VPN configurations, subscription URLs, proxy links, virtual adapter settings, and local logs that you import or create. This data is used to start VPN sessions, show connection status, import or export configurations, and diagnose connection issues.

iCloud Drive Backups

If you choose to use iCloud Drive backup or restore, configuration backups are saved into your own iCloud Drive container. We do not have access to your iCloud Drive content.

Camera and Photos

SpellVPN requests camera access only to scan QR codes for importing VPN configurations or subscription links. When you import a QR code from Photos, the app processes the image you select in order to detect QR content.

Network Connections

SpellVPN does not provide built-in VPN servers, sell proxy nodes, or host user traffic. Your imported configuration may contain subscription URLs, DNS servers, proxy servers, or other network endpoints. During a connection, the app and Packet Tunnel extension access those endpoints according to the configuration you provide.

Logs

The app may generate local connection logs, validation messages, and diagnostic logs to help troubleshoot issues. Logs stay on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Children's Privacy

SpellVPN is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If a future version adds accounts, developer-operated cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, payments, advertising, or other data collection, this policy and the App Store privacy labels will be updated.
