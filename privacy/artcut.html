ArtCut Privacy Policy

A local-first video editor for timeline stitching, trimming, captions, reverse clips, transitions, overlays, audio, and export.

ArtCut Support

Data We Collect

The current version of ArtCut does not require an account, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not track users across apps or websites.

Media, Drafts, and Caches

Imported videos, project drafts, export files, reversed clip renders, extracted audio, automatic caption results, and generated caches are stored on your device by default.

Photos

ArtCut may request access to Photos so you can import videos and save exported videos.

Camera and Microphone

If capture features are enabled in the final app, ArtCut may request camera and microphone access so you can record video or audio for editing.

Speech Recognition

Automatic captions use Apple's speech recognition framework when you explicitly start caption generation.

Sharing and Export

When you export, save, or share a video, the selected output may be handled by Apple system services or by apps and services you choose from the share sheet.

Children's Privacy

ArtCut is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If a future version adds accounts, developer-operated cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, payments, advertising, or other data collection, this policy and the App Store privacy labels will be updated.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support.github.io.
