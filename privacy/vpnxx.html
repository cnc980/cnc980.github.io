VPN XX Privacy Policy

A subscription and node manager for proxy links, QR imports, iCloud sync, favorites, and endpoint speed testing.

VPN XX Support

Data You Provide

VPN XX stores subscription URLs, proxy links, imported node details, node names, favorites, speed-test status, and related settings that you enter, scan, paste, import, or create in the app.

Local Storage and iCloud

Subscription and node data is stored on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled and available, the app can sync this data through your private iCloud account using CloudKit.

Subscription Refresh and Speed Testing

When you refresh a subscription, VPN XX connects to the subscription URL you provided. When you test node speed or reachability, the app may connect to endpoints contained in your imported nodes.

Advertising in the Free Edition

The free edition of VPN XX can display ads and is prepared to use Yandex Mobile Ads with Google AdMob mediation. Depending on your region and consent choices, these SDKs may process device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app interaction data, and diagnostics for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and compliance.

Pro Edition

VPN XX Pro is a paid edition that does not show ads.

No Developer-Provided VPN Network

VPN XX does not sell VPN nodes, provide proxy subscriptions, or operate a VPN traffic network.

Children's Privacy

VPN XX is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If a future version adds accounts, developer-operated cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, payments, advertising, or other data collection, this policy and the App Store privacy labels will be updated.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@your-domain.example.
