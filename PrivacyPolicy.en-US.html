Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

ClipCraft is a local-first video editing app. Review and update this template with your legal name, contact email, support URL, and final product behavior before release.

Data We Collect

The current version does not require an account and does not include advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking. Imported media, drafts, export files, reversed clip renders, extracted audio, and generated caches are stored on the user's device by default.

If you add analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, accounts, payments, ads, server-side transcription, or third-party SDKs, update this policy and App Store privacy answers before submission.

Photos, Camera, Microphone, and Speech Recognition

The app may request access to Photos so users can import videos and save exported videos. Camera and microphone descriptions are included for capture-oriented workflows if enabled in the final app. Automatic captions use Apple's speech recognition framework when the user explicitly starts caption generation.

Local Drafts and Caches

Drafts and generated media caches are stored locally on the device. Users can clear generated caches from the app's menu.

Data Sharing

The current implementation does not upload projects to a developer-operated server and does not sell user data. Standard Apple system services may process data when users grant permission, such as speech recognition and Photos operations.
